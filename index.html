The Beginning

The "Quiet" Before the Storm

Yaar, sab wahin se shuru hua. BBC building. Anshika aur Pranshu apni hi duniya me baatein kar rahe the, aur hum dono ke beech wo... Cute, awkward silence... Kuch bol nahi rahe the par dil zor-zor se dhadak raha tha. Main soch raha tha ki tum kya soch rahi ho. Fir tumne mera phone liya, thoda aage aayi aur ye photo click ki. Sach batau? Ghar jaate hue pure raste auto me bas isi photo ko ghoor raha tha main. Ye humari pehli nishani thi.

Day 1: The Vision in Black

Anubhuti ka pehla din. Tumhara call aaya: "Kya pehen ke aaye ho?" Maine normal sa bola "Black shirt." Par mujhe kya pata tha ki tum kya karne wali ho.

Jab tum 3 baje aayi na... *kasam se*, sab gayab ho gaye meri nazron se. Itni bheed thi par mujhe bas tum dikh rahi thi. Black dress me tum... unreal lag rahi thi. Sukoon. Bas wahi mila tumhe dekh ke. Maine usi pal soch liya tha, ki bas... ab toh yahi chahiye. I knew I was gone for you.

The First Frame

Raat ho rahi thi Gate pe. Mujhe bas ek photo chahiye thi tumhare sath, kaise bhi karke. Jab hum finally sath khade hue, toh sab dost chidane lage... "Umm hmm, acche lag rahe dono." Tum sharma rahi thi, aur main? Main toh andar hi andar foola nahi sama raha tha. Wo humari pehli 'official' photo thi sath me. Is moment ko main kabhi nahi bhul sakta.

The Feelings

Holi Butterflies

Bhai planning full thi meri. Soch ke aaya tha ki sabse pehle rang main hi lagaunga tumhe. B2 ke bahar wait karte hue heart-beat badh rahi thi. Bhale hi pehla number mera nahi aaya, par jab maine tumhe chua... Literal chills... current laga tha mujhe. Tum baar-baar rang saaf kar rahi thi, aur main bahane dhund raha tha wapas lagane ke... bas tumhe chhune ke liye. Wo nasha hi alag tha.

The Cheek Squeeze

B1 ke bahar, wo laal pipe ke paas. Yaar tum isme itni *pyaari* lag rahi thi na, mujhse control hi nahi hua. Maine himmat jutayi aur tumhare gaal pakad liye. Chahe hazaar log khade ho, meri nazar bas tumpe rehti hai. Sach batau? Mann kar raha tha tumhe kha jau. You looked so innocent and beautiful.

Core Memory

Bahana tha laptop repair ka, par mission tha tumhe ghar lana. Bike pe jab tum piche baithi aur apna haath mere kandhe pe rakha... Core Memory Unlocked. Aisa laga duniya jeet li maine. Mummy ke saamne tum darr rahi thi (aur baad me mujhe daant bhi padi!), par wo kuch pal jo humne mere room me bitaye... *worth it* tha sab kuch. Laga ki haan, ye meri family hai.

The Promise

11/07/2025

Wo pal jisne sab badal diya. Tumne pucha: "Will you be my boyfriend?...mtlb abhi kch sal fir saadi kr hii lenge..."

Mera jawab bas 'Haan' nahi tha, ek wada tha. Maine kaha tha na: "Baby feelings genuine hai... wife banaunga tumhe... guarantee de sakta hu, samajh rahi ho na bubu?" Aaj bhi wahi guarantee hai. Life set ho gayi thi us din.

Reunion

Mission: Reunion

Tum Dhampur gayi thi toh yahan sab suna-suna lag raha tha. Par jab tum wapas aayi... J&H Cafe me. Tumne mere kandhe pe sar rakha aur mujhe laga jaan me jaan aayi. Sukoon. Aur fir wo 'Mission Impossible' tumhe ghar lane ka! Camera off karna, padosiyon se chup ke aana... darr nahi lag raha tha, bas *thrill* tha ki tum mere paas ho. Best feeling ever.

Date Night

30/07/2025. Eco Garden ke momos aur Zorko. Jagah simple thi, par *mahaul* alag tha kyunki tum sath thi. Tumhari aankhon me wo chamak dekh ke main wahi pighal gaya tha. Momos khate hue bhi koi itna sundar lag sakta hai kya? Us shaam mujhe tumse dubara pyaar ho gaya tha.
